# ClockTray: Quick Clock Control at Your Fingertips

## What Is ClockTray?

ClockTray is a lightweight Windows utility that gives you instant control over your taskbar's date and time display. One click (or hotkey) hides it when you need screen real estate, and one click brings it back. No menus, no settings windows, no fuss.

## The Problem It Solves

Windows' taskbar clock occupies valuable screen space, especially on smaller monitors or high-resolution displays where taskbar real estate is precious. You can't easily toggle it off without digging through Settings—until now. ClockTray puts that power in your hands instantly.

**Think of it like this:** Your taskbar clock is a light switch that was locked in the wall. ClockTray puts a remote control in your pocket.

## Key Features

✨ **One-Click Toggle** — Hide or show your taskbar clock instantly  
⚡ **Hotkey Shortcut** — Default: Ctrl+Alt+T (customizable)  
🔧 **No Dependencies** — Runs lean; doesn't bloat your system  
🪟 **Windows 10 & 11** — Works across Windows versions  
🎚️ **Easy Settings** — Customize hotkeys directly from PowerToys settings  

## Before & After ClockTray

### Before
1. Taskbar is cluttered with clock you don't always need
2. Hiding it requires: Settings → Personalization → Taskbar → Turn off the clock (buried 3 levels deep)
3. Toggling back and forth is painful
4. No global hotkey option

### After
1. Press **Ctrl+Alt+T** (or your custom hotkey)
2. Clock disappears (or reappears)
3. Takes 1 second
4. Customizable hotkey for power users

## Who Should Use It?

🎯 **Power users** who work across multiple displays or full-screen apps and want to reclaim taskbar space  
🎯 **Productivity enthusiasts** who streamline their desktop for focus  
🎯 **Keyboard-first workers** who prefer hotkeys over mouse clicks  
🎯 **Anyone** who finds the taskbar clock occasionally intrusive  

## Quick Start

1. Install ClockTray from PowerToys (included with the utility suite)
2. Enable it in PowerToys Settings → ClockTray
3. (Optional) Customize your hotkey
4. Press your hotkey to toggle the clock on/off

That's it. Your taskbar, your rules.
